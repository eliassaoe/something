Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how LinkedIn Sales Navigator Scraper Extension ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Chrome extension for LinkedIn Sales Navigator.

2. Information We Collect

Our extension collects and processes the following types of data:

2.1 LinkedIn Data

  • Profile Information: Names, job titles, company names, locations, and other publicly available profile data from LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  • Contact Information: Email addresses and phone numbers when available through LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  • Search Results: Data from your LinkedIn Sales Navigator searches and filters

2.2 Usage Data

  • Extension Usage: Information about how you interact with our extension (clicks, features used, time spent)
  • Technical Data: Browser type, version, and basic system information necessary for extension functionality

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To extract and organize LinkedIn Sales Navigator data as requested by you
  • Data Export: To provide you with downloadable lead lists in various formats (CSV, Excel, etc.)
  • Extension Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve extension performance
  • Customer Support: To provide technical support and respond to your inquiries

4. Data Storage and Processing

  • Local Storage: Most data is processed and stored locally on your device
  • Temporary Processing: Some data may be temporarily processed on our servers for email enrichment services
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your extracted LinkedIn data on our servers
  • Data Retention: Usage analytics are retained for up to 12 months for improvement purposes

5. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Third-Party Services: We may use email verification services to enhance contact data accuracy
  • Service Providers: We may share data with technical service providers who assist in extension functionality
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfer: Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

Our extension may integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Email Verification Services: To validate and enrich email addresses
  • Analytics Services: To understand extension usage and performance
  • LinkedIn: Our extension operates within LinkedIn Sales Navigator's interface

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data from our systems
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics collection through extension settings
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension at any time to stop all data collection
