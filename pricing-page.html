Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between plans?

Starter gives you unlimited exports with daily limits for fair usage. Pro gives you unlimited exports with higher daily limits plus unlimited email finder, email verifier, and LinkedIn scraping.

What are the daily export limits?

Starter plan: Up to 5,000 leads per day (~150,000/month).
Pro plan: Up to 10,000 leads per day (~300,000/month).

Both limits are more than enough for most sales teams and reset daily.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, cancel anytime. No contracts. You keep access until your current billing period ends.

How accurate is the data?

95% email deliverability rate with verified contacts and real-time validation. Our database is updated weekly to ensure accuracy.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! Every new user gets 250 free verified leads upon sign-up. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express) through our secure Stripe payment processor.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll pro-rate the difference.
