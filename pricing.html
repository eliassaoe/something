Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about UnlimitedLeads

How do one-time payments work?

With UnlimitedLeads, you pay once and get lifetime access to our platform. There are no monthly fees, annual renewals, or hidden costs. You'll continue to have access to the platform, including new leads added to our database, for as long as we operate.

How are your leads verified?

We use a multi-step verification process to ensure email deliverability. This includes syntax checking, domain verification, and mailbox verification. Our system continuously monitors and updates contact information to maintain accuracy.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade from the Starter Plan to the All Access Plan at any time. You'll only pay the difference between the two plans. Contact our support team when you're ready to upgrade.

What type of data is included with each lead?

Each lead includes full name, job title, email address, phone number (when available), company name, company size, industry, location, and social media profiles links (when available).

Do you offer a refund policy?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first week, contact our support team for a full refund.

How many leads can I export each month?

The Starter Plan allows for 5,000 lead exports per month, while the All Access Plan includes 10,000 monthly exports. These export limits reset at the beginning of each month.
