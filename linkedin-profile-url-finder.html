How to Use Our LinkedIn Profile URL Finder

Our comprehensive tool helps you find LinkedIn profile URLs for both people and companies, offering single searches for quick lookups or bulk processing for large datasets.

Person Profile Search

  1. Enter person details: Provide first name and last name (required), plus company name (optional for better accuracy).
  2. Click "Find LinkedIn Profile": Our tool searches LinkedIn's database to find the matching profile.
  3. Get instant results: See the LinkedIn profile URL with a direct link to visit the profile.

Company Profile Search

  1. Enter company name: Type the company name you want to find on LinkedIn.
  2. Search company LinkedIn: Our system locates the official company LinkedIn page.
  3. Access company profile: Get the LinkedIn company page URL with direct access link.

Bulk CSV Processing

  1. Prepare your CSV: For people: columns named "first_name", "last_name", "company_name". For companies: column named "company_name".
  2. Upload your file: Drag and drop your CSV or click to browse and select it.
  3. Preview and process: Review the first 5 rows, then click "Process" to find LinkedIn profiles for all entries.
  4. Download results: Export your results as a new CSV file with names, LinkedIn URLs, and status.
